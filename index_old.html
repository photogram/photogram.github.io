Team

how we became

We are Photogram, a computational photography startup based in London. We specialise in AI-accelerated hardware and software for modern photography and for content creators.
‍
We were founded on the Entrepreneur First LD12 cohort in July 2019 but have now incubated at UCL Innovation & Enterprise’s Hatchery and is part of the NVIDIA Inception Program for AI start-ups revolutionising industries. We are at the intersection of computer vision, artificial intelligence, IoT, as well as creativity, photography and consumer goods.
