A little about us

Our approach is to build the camera of the future: an AI-powered camera that's more effective at creating content than any camera.

This camera will have effortless control, instant access to files and a low-barrier to entry. Using a combination of pro-optics and AI, it will make your content look high-quality and ready to share straight out of camera.

Photogram was founded by Vishal Kumar, Liam Donovan, and Vik Kumar. We are now group of ten researchers, engineers and content creators. Our team has built and shipped consumer hardware products with integrated operating system software and AI.
